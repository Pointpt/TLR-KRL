Case name: Request Identity Card
Participating actors: Started by Citizen, continued employee
Flow of events:
1. The Citizen displays the family status and selects a family member.
2. The system shows a form for entering personal data (Height, special signs, etc.)
3. The citizen enters the data, selects the mode of payment and presses the confirmation button.
4. The system shows the screen with the documentation to be shown at the counter on the days and times of office opening.
5. The citizen confirms the operation. 
6. The system notifies the employee of the request.
Entry condition:
The citizen is authenticated in the system.
The citizen exercises the homeland potest affect that.
Exit conditions:
The employee receives the file and chooses whether to accept it or not, recalling the use case respectively Identity Card Request Rejected Identity Card Request Accepted, OR
If the service is not available, the use case is called up Service Not Available.
