Case name: Edit EMail Employee
Participating actors: Started as an employee
Flow of events: 
1. The employee accesses the e-mail modification form.
2. The system shows the form the insertion of the new e-mail.
3. The employee enters the new email and presses the confirmation button.
4. The system updates the new e-mail.
Entry condition:
The employee is authenticated in the system.
Exit conditions:
The system saves the new e-mail.
The system detects an error and recalls the use case data OR
In case the service is not available the use case is recalled Service Not Available.
Quality requirements:
Not expected.
