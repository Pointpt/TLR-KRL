Case name: Identity Card Request Accepted
Participating actors: Started by Employee, continued citizen
Flow of events:
1. The employee displays the notification received.
2. The system shows the employee the request to create the identity card from part of the citizen.
3. The employee certifies the validity of the request by checking the accuracy of the data and enables the procedure for creating the identity card.
4. The system sends a confirmation e-mail to the citizen who requested the creation of the identity card. 
5. The citizen goes to the counter.
Entry condition:
The employee is authenticated in the system
(This use case extends Identity Card Request) and occurs when the employee confirms the request of the citizen.
Exit conditions:
The system alerts the citizen.
Quality requirements:
Not expected.
