Case name: Request DateIdentit Rejected
Participating actors: Started by Employee, continued citizen
Flow of events:
1. The employee displays the notification received.
2. The system shows the employee the request to create the identity card from part of the citizen.
3. The employee detects an error in the data and refuses the procedure for creating the identity card.
4. The system sends an e-mail notification to the citizen who requested the creation of the identity card.
Entry condition:
The employee is authenticated in the system.
(This use case extends Identity Card Request) and occurs when the employee does not confirm the citizen's request.
Exit conditions:
The system alerts the citizen.
Quality requirements:
Not expected.
