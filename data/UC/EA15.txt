Case name: Edit Employee
Participating actors: Started by administrator
Flow of events:
1. The administrator presses the change employee button.
2. The system shows a form for modifying the employee.
3. The administrator enters the data. 
4. The system saves the changes made.
Entry condition:
The administrator has authenticated himself in the system.
(This case of use includes Employee Search) and occurs when the administrator wants to modify an employee
Exit conditions:
The system modifies the employee, OR
The case is called if the service is not available. Service Not Available.
Quality requirements:
Not expected.
