Case name: Ticket request
Participating actors: Started by Citizen, continued as a clerk
Flow of events:
1. The Citizen presses the Electoral Card button.
2. The system shows the form to request the card.
3. The citizen confirms the operation 
4. The system sends a notification clerk. 
5. The citizen will receive the electoral card will be delivered to your home.
Entry condition:
Citizen must be authenticated in the system. The electoral card has not been sent.
Exit conditions:
The system alerts the employee, OR
The case is called if the service is not available. Service Not Available.
Quality requirements:
Not expected.
