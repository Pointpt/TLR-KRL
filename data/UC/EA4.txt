Case name: CancellationCitizen
Participating actors: Started as an employee
Flow of events: 
1. The employee presses the citizen cancellation button.
2. The system asks the employee for confirmation.
3. The employee confirms the operation.
4. The system cancels the citizen.
Entry condition: 
The employee is authenticated in the system.
(This use case includes Research Citizen) and occurs when the employee wants to cancel a citizen
Exit conditions: 
The employee does not confirm the operation, OR
The system cancels the citizen, OR
The case is called if the service is not available. Service Not Available
Quality requirements:
Not expected
