Case name: EditPractice
Participating actors: Initiated by a citizen
Flow of events:
1. The citizen presses the practice modification button (identity card, change residence) if the case has not been initialized.
2. The system shows a form for editing the file.
3. The citizen enters the data.
4. The system saves the changes made.
Entry condition:
The citizen is authenticated in the system.
(This case extends VisualizzaPratica) and occurs when the citizen wants to change the practice.
Exit conditions:
The system modifies the practice, OR
The case is called if the service is not available. Service Not Available.
Quality requirements:
Not expected.
