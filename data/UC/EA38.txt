Case name: Loss of Paper and Identity
Participating actors: Started by Citizen, continued as a clerk
Flow of events:
1. The Citizen presses the button Loss of identity card.
2. The system sends a notification of loss of the card directly to the competent employee.
3. The citizen goes to the clerk's office with a copy of the loss report.
Entry condition:
Citizen must be authenticated in the system.
Exit conditions:
The System creates the document to be delivered to the competent office, OR
In case the service is not available the use case is recalled Service Not Available.
Quality requirements:
Not expected.
