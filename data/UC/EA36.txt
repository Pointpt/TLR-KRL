Case name: request family state
Participating actors: Started by Citizen
Flow of events: 
1. The Citizen presses the Family Status button.
2. The system shows the family the status of the family.
3. The citizen chooses whether to print or send to his e-mail the family status.
4. The system makes the choice made by the citizen.
Entry condition:
The Citizen is authenticated in the system.
Exit conditions:
The system performs an operation chosen by the citizen OR,
If the service is not available, the use case is called up Service Not Available.
Quality requirements:
Not expected.
