Case name: DisplayPractice
Participating actors: Initiated by a citizen
Flow of events: 
1. Citizens click on the view files button (Identity Card e0, Change Residence).
2. The system displays a screen with the practices concerning the citizen.
Entry condition:
The citizen is authenticated in the system.
Exit conditions:
The citizen displays the screen, OR
If the citizen wants to cancel a file, the system calls the case of use Clear Practice, OR
The system calls in the case in which the citizen wants to modify a practice the case of use Edit Practice, OR
The case is called if the service is not available. Service Not Available.
Quality requirements:
Not Expected
